# BOM Reconciliation Requirements - Synthetic

Compare the controlled drawing parts list with the procurement bill of
materials.

Requirements:

- Match records by exact part number.
- Identify parts present on the drawing but absent from the procurement BOM.
- Identify parts present on the procurement BOM but absent from the drawing.
- Identify quantity differences for part numbers present in both documents.
- Identify revision differences for part numbers present in both documents.
- Do not treat description wording as a substitute for the part number.
- Reject the package for correction when any discrepancy exists.
- Do not approve or release procurement or fabrication documents.
